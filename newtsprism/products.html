Prism Research Products

In an effort to save our natural resources, Prism Research offers their products in electronic form over the Internet. If needed we will ship a diskette, and/or hardcopy of the documents to the individual at no extra cost.

Bulk discounts available upon request.

Use nsScribe to write your homework on the Notepad or Newton Works. Do calculus, explore complex math, manipulate matrices, fit your data to any function. Generate random variables, analyze statistical data, find the best distribution.

    • Use Works Calculations to graph, take symbolic derivatives, and most of what MathLib alone can do.
