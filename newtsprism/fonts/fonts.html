Free Newton Fonts*

The following files contain various fonts that were ported to the Newton.  Included in the folder are the original fonts and descriptions from the original authors and the ported package.  The original fonts are for the Macintosh. Some of the originals are shareware and some are freeware. If you downloaded the earlier versions, then you must delete the old packages from your Newton before loading these new ones. The developer signiture was added to the package names.
